How this works: all 216 = 65,536 microstates are weighted by their Boltzmann factor e−E/kBT. Each draw picks 16 states independently from that distribution. The probability shown on each card is P(s) = e−E/kBT / Z, where Z is the exact partition function. At low T the sample is dominated by ordered ground states; at high T disordered configurations flood in.

Ising energy: E = −J Σ⟨ij⟩ sisj (nearest-neighbour, periodic boundary conditions, J = 1). The 2D critical temperature is kBTc / J ≈ 2.269.
